History

Hope Community Resource Center (HCRC) has been a 501(c)3 non-profit incorporated in the State of Louisiana since 2010. Our work has been focused on two areas: Helping poor urban youth benefit from frequent participation in quality education and enrichment programming. Assisting students in the New Orleans Metropolitan area including the Gentilly and New Orleans East communities develop the educational foundation that they need.



We achieve success in these two areas by providing the following programs:

  • Resources for Parents and Children
  • Tutoring
  • Parent Involvement Workshops
  • Parent Training
  • Technology
  • Summer Programs
  • Adult Literacy Program
  • Mentoring Programs
  • Field Trips
