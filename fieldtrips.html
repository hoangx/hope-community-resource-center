Field Trips

The Hope Community Resource Center cultural field trips are free and open to everyone. We strive to expose our students to the history and the beauty of New Orleans culture. Every third Saturday of the month the Hope Community Resource Center visits a different museum in the New Orleans area.


Please see our calendar on our homepage for a full schedule of the events.
