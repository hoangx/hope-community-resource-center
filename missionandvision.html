Mission

The Hope Community Resource Center is a partnership among, parents, youth, and the community committed to nurturing, supporting, educating, and strengthening family life through quality child, youth, and family development. The Center serves the New Orleans metropolitan area (New Orleans East families) and facilitates child and family issues with an emphasis on community collaboration.



Vision

At Hope Community Resource Center, we are dedicated to the empowerment of families and youth through partnerships with family and community to facilitate learning which fosters social and success, producing lifelong learners, responsible citizens, and positively impacting the future generation.



Super Service Challenge

Here is a video for the the "Super Service Challenge" with two mentors describing what volunteering at the Center means to them. History of the Super Service Challenge: In 2011, Dave Lindsey, founder of DEFENDER, an Indianapolis-based service company, created the Super Service Challenge. The Challenge helped direct company donations to the projects and causes that mattered to his employees, in a meaningful way. Serving together changed the way employees viewed each other and had a positive impact on morale. DEFENDER transformed from a giving company to a company of givers.
